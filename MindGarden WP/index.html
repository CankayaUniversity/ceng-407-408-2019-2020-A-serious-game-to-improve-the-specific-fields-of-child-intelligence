Welcome to Mind Garden

Mind Garden is a serious game. Our vision is to use entertainment to improve learning process of children. We aim to create a platform containing various of serious games which will address different parts of mental attributes with different learning styles. We are from Cankaya University and Mind Garden is our senior project.
